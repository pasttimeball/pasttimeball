Yellville had a game lined up for the Fourth of July, 1905, until the Walnut Ridge team "flunked on the local team" too late to find a replacement. No game on the biggest baseball day of the year.

The Echo's postmortem is five words of perfect small-town scorn: Guess they got "afeard." The quotation marks are the paper's own, which somehow makes it worse for Walnut Ridge.
