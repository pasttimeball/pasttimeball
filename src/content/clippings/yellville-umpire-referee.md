The whole item: "Ben Ward says it takes a good man to umpire a ball game and referee a fight at the same time; but that he did it last Tuesday."

That is everything the Mountain Echo felt its readers needed. What the fight was about, who was in it, whether it was related to the ball game, all left to the imagination. The paper trusted that everyone in town already knew, and it was probably right.
